Security Architecture

Designed for Security

Every layer of SecureVault is engineered to protect your data from the moment it leaves your device.

AES-256-GCM Encryption

Authenticated encryption that guarantees both confidentiality and integrity. Each file gets a unique key derived via PBKDF2 with a random IV — never reused.

PBKDF2 Key Derivation

600,000 iterations of PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256 stretch your password into a cryptographic key, making offline brute-force attacks computationally infeasible.

Integrity Verification

GCM authentication tags verify every download. If a single byte is tampered with, decryption is aborted before any plaintext is exposed to your system.

Zero Plaintext Storage

Plaintext is encrypted in-memory and immediately discarded. Only ciphertext bytes ever touch the disk or database — never the original content.

Brute-Force Protection

Rate-limited login endpoints and automatic account lockout after 5 failed attempts prevent password guessing and credential stuffing attacks.

Immutable Audit Trail

Every login, upload, download, and delete is logged with timestamp and IP address for complete forensic accountability and compliance review.
